Style Inspiration: Alexa Chung

Born in Privett, Hampshire, Chung is the daughter of Gillian and Phillip Chung. Chung is a muse to many fashion designers because of her distinctive personal style. She frequently appears on best-dressed lists, is a regular model for Vogue, Elle and Harper's Bazaar and is often seen in the front row at fashion shows.

In 2009, the designer handbag company Mulberry created the "Alexa", a much sought-after bag named after and inspired by her. In January 2010, she was named in Tatler's top 10 best-dressed list. In February 2010, Chung collaborated with J.Crew's Madewell on a womenswear line which was unveiled during New York Fashion Week. She collaborated with Madewell for a second collection, released in September 2011. At the British Fashion Awards of 2011, 2012 and 2013, Chung won the British Style Award, for which the public voted. In 2015, Chung collaborated with AG Jeans on two collections. Read More
